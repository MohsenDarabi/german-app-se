## Asset Specifications

### Images
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 800x600 (landscape) or 600x800 (portrait)
- **Style**: Friendly, diverse, modern, educational
- **Save to**: `apps/web/static/images/{lesson-id}/`

### Videos
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution**: 1080p or 720p
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds
- **Audio**: Clear German pronunciation
- **Save to**: `apps/web/static/videos/{lesson-id}/`
